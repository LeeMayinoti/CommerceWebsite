about us


Handcrafted luxury skincare. Nashe Naturals is a business dedicated to guilt free self-care. Using natural and plant-based ingredients to cater to your skincare needs and provide glorious feeling products that make you feel comfortable, sexy and confident in your own skin.

Scrubs

Our basic body scrubs are designed to exfoliate and moisturize. Each with their own additional benefits, these scrubs will leave your skin refreshed, hydrated and smooth. The flavours include; Cinnamon Latte, Chia Mint Chip, Blue Raspberry and Strawberry Rose.

Body Creams

Our body creams are perfect for everyone, especially those with dry skin. These will moisturize, soothe and rejuvenate the skin leaving it silky smooth for the whole day. Flavours include; Vanilla Chai, Peppermint, Sandalwood & Eucalyptus and Bergamot & Cinnamon.

Body wash

Our luxurious body washes will help cleanse the skin without drying it out and leave you smelling fantastic. Perfect for your everyday shower. Flavours include; Sweet Cherry, Mint Apple, Honey Lavender and Citrus.
